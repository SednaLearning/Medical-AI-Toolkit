# Riskmanagementreport Assessment

## Kontext
We are a small start up that is developing a medical Class IIb device according to the EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR).

The document under review is the Risk Management Report, which summarizes the implementation of the risk management process according to ISO 14971.

---

## Ziel
Perform a structured compliance gap analysis between the provided Risk Management Report and the requirements defined in the ISO 14971 checklist.
The goal is to identify missing, incomplete or insufficiently justified elements that could lead to a regulatory finding.
The assessment shall focus on the adequacy of the documented risk management process and not on the technical design of the medical device itself.
---

## Rolle
Act as an experienced medical device regulatory reviewer familiar with:
- EU MDR requirements
- ISO 14971 principles
- regulatory expectations of European competent authorities
- notified body audit practices
The reviewer shall take a conservative regulatory perspective and request objective evidence for compliance.
---

## Aufgabe
1. read the provided checklist for the requierments according to ISO 14971
2. Read the provided Risk Management Report
3. Analyze the structure and scope of the Risk Management Report.
4. Map each checklist requirement to relevant sections of the report. For each requirement evaluate:
- presence of documented evidence
- adequacy of the evidence
- consistency with the requirement
5. Evaluate whether the requirement is:
   - fulfilled
   - partially fulfilled
   - not fulfilled
6. Only classify partially fulfilled or not fulfilled requirements as findings.
7. For each findings of the risk management report
a.	Classify as major or minor finding
b.	Reference the original passage in the report with page number
c.	State the finding
d.	Give a source for the justification of the deviation (law / regulation / guideline with section / paragraph) Only reference sources that are explicitly available in the provided checklist or reference documents.
Do not create or guess clause numbers.
e.	Make a proposal for correcting the finding

---

## Regeln
Use english language

Major finding:
A missing or inadequate element that could significantly affect patient safety, regulatory compliance or the effectiveness of the risk management process.

Minor finding:
A limited deficiency that does not significantly impact the overall effectiveness of the risk management process.
If the severity cannot be determined based on available evidence, classify as "unclear" and explain why.
Only report findings where the evidence in the Risk Management Report clearly demonstrates a deviation from the checklist requirement.
Do not report:
- general comments
- recommendations
- improvements
- assumptions
Assess compliance only based on the information available in the provided Risk Management Report.

If a requirement cannot reasonably be demonstrated by this document alone, classify it as:
"Not assessable based on the provided document."
Do not classify such cases as non-conformities unless the report is expected to contain the required information.
---

## Gewünschtes Ergebnis
please provide a table with the following colums:
Requirement; Classification of Finding (minor/major); reference of original passage in the riskmanagement report incl. page number; Finding; source for the justification of the deviation (law / regulation / guideline with section / paragraph); proposal for correcting the finding

## Beispiele

### Example 1: Missing risk acceptance criteria

Input evidence:

Risk Management Report:
"The identified risks were evaluated and controlled."

Assessment:

Requirement:
Risk acceptance criteria shall be defined before risk evaluation.

Classification:
Major

Finding:
The report does not provide evidence of predefined risk acceptance criteria.

Source:
ISO 14971:2019 Clause 5

Proposal:
Add documented risk acceptance criteria and demonstrate their application.

### Example 2: Evidence is missing
Requirement:
Risk control effectiveness shall be verified.

Evidence:
The report states verification was performed.

Assessment:
Partially fulfilled

Finding:
Verification activities are referenced but objective evidence is not provided.
...

## Hinweise

Do not assume information exists outside the provided documents.
If required information is not available, state:
"Not demonstrated in the provided documentation."
Do not invent missing evidence.
